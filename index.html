01 Who I am

Building Africa's intelligent infrastructure.

I'm a data scientist and AI engineer based in Harare, Zimbabwe — working at the intersection of enterprise data systems, machine learning, and AI agent development. My work involves reverse-engineering complex ERP databases, building production AI agents, and shipping full-stack data applications for clients with real operational needs.

At AI Creative Solutions, my scope spans data science, AI agent architecture, and full software systems — we turn raw enterprise data into decisions, from inventory traceability and production intelligence to sales analytics and operational efficiency.

Beyond my core role, I serve as Zindi Ambassador for Zimbabwe — organising data science events, running competitions, and mentoring practitioners across the country. I write about technology and leadership for Right for Education (R:Ed). I believe the best AI solutions for Africa will be built by people who genuinely understand its terrain.
