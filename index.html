01Who I am

Building Africa's intelligent infrastructure.

I'm a data scientist and AI engineer based in Harare, Zimbabwe — working at the intersection of enterprise data systems, machine learning, and AI agent development. My work involves designing intelligent systems on complex operational data, building production AI agents, and shipping full-stack applications for clients with real business needs.

At AI Creative Solutions, my scope spans data science, AI agent architecture, and full software engineering — turning raw enterprise data into decisions, from operational intelligence and production tracking to sales analytics and automated reporting.

Beyond my core role, I serve as Zindi Ambassador for Zimbabwe — organising data science events, running competitions, and mentoring practitioners across the country. I write about technology and leadership for Right for Education (R:Ed), reaching audiences across Africa. I believe the best AI solutions for Africa will be built by people who truly understand its terrain.
